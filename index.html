About Restaurent

Palleturi Tiffins - Home-Made South Indian Delights

Welcome to Palleturi Tiffins, where we serve fresh, wholesome, and delicious South Indian tiffins (snacks) made from the finest ingredients with a homemade touch. At Palleturi Tiffins, we bring the authentic taste of South India straight to your table. Whether you're a fan of soft idlis, crispy dosas, or savory puris, we have something to satisfy your cravings.

The Unique Touch - Home-Made Tiffins

At Palleturi Tiffins, we take pride in our homemade approach. We believe in providing food that not only tastes good but feels like a warm meal made by your mother or grandmother. Our tiffins are freshly made in-house, following age-old recipes passed down through generations, ensuring an authentic, comforting experience every time you eat with us.

No Preservatives:We use fresh, natural ingredients with no added preservatives or artificial flavors, ensuring that every meal is as wholesome as it is tasty.

Traditional Recipes:Every dish is made from scratch, using traditional South Indian recipes to maintain the authentic taste and texture of the tiffins.

Love in Every Bite:The secret ingredient is love. Every dish at Palleturi Tiffins is made with care and passion for food, making sure you feel at home with every bite.

Why Choose Palleturi Tiffins?

Authentic Homemade Taste:Our food is made with care, just like how it would be prepared in a South Indian home.

Healthy & Wholesome:Freshly prepared daily, our tiffins are nutritious and provide the perfect balance of taste and health.

Customizable Dishes:You can customize your tiffins according to your preferences. Choose your spice levels or opt for additional chutneys and sides!

Quick and Convenient:Whether you're in a rush or looking to enjoy a leisurely meal, our dishes are served quickly without compromising on quality or flavor.
